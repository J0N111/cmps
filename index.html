Great to meet you!

As a Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW), I believe that with the right support, you can overcome whatever obstacles you’re facing. I have a deep passion for supporting teenagers, young adults (20-35), and individuals from all walks of life.

A bit about my approach to therapy

Managing complicated family dynamics, school stress, or past trauma can sometimes leave you feeling stuck and overwhelmed. I’m here to help you navigate these challenges and discover your inner strength. I use a myriad of treatment modalities, and collaboratively we will identify which method is best suited for your needs.

What you can expect from our first session

During our initial session, we explore your unique strengths and struggles to outline meaningful steps that will help you reach your goals. Earning your trust is essential, and I strive to create a safe and supportive environment where you can feel empowered to make lasting change. I am excited to partner with you to unlock your full potential and help you live a life of meaning and purpose.
